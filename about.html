About Us

Chhavi Industries is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality inks, specializing in both water-based and solvent-based inks for industrial, commercial, and packaging applications. With years of expertise in the printing industry, we are committed to delivering innovative, eco-friendly, and reliable solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and performance.

Our mission is to provide top-quality inks that empower businesses to create vibrant, durable, and sustainable printed products. We strive to combine innovation, reliability, and environmental responsibility in every product we deliver.

To be a trusted name in the printing and ink industry, known for excellence, sustainability, and customer satisfaction worldwide.

Water-Based Inks: Eco-friendly inks for paper, textiles, and packaging with vibrant colors and smooth finishes.

Solvent-Based Inks: Durable inks for plastics, metals, glass, and non-porous surfaces with excellent adhesion and longevity.

Customized Solutions: Tailored formulations to meet your specific printing needs.

✅ Quality Assurance: Rigorous testing and high standards for every batch of ink.

✅ Innovation: Constantly improving formulations to meet modern printing requirements.

✅ Eco-Friendly Practices: Commitment to sustainable and safe ink production.

✅ Customer Support: Dedicated team to ensure your complete satisfaction.

At Chhavi Industries, we believe in building long-term relationships with our clients by delivering reliable, high-performance, and eco-conscious printing solutions. We aim to help your business stand out with colors that last and inks you can trust.
