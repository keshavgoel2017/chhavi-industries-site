Solvent-Based Inks

Solvent-based inks are high-performance inks that use organic solvents to carry pigments. These inks are known for their excellent adhesion, durability, and resistance to water, chemicals, and fading. They are ideal for printing on non-porous surfaces like plastics, metals, glass, and coated materials.

✅Key Benefits: Durable & Long-Lasting: Resistant to water, UV light, and abrasion, making prints highly durable.

✅Excellent Adhesion: Works on a wide variety of surfaces, including non-porous materials where water-based inks may fail.

Vibrant & Sharp Colors: Produces strong, vivid colors with consistent print quality.

✅Fast Drying: Reduces production time and improves efficiency in commercial printing.

✅Versatile Applications: Suitable for industrial, packaging, and specialty printing.

✅Applications: Solvent-based inks are commonly used in: Flexible packaging and labels,Plastic, metal, and glass printing Industrial and commercial printing Outdoor signage and billboards.
